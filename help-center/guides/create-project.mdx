---
title: Creating a New am Project
description: Step-by-step to start your first documentation space with am.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up or log in">
    Go to `https://am.com` and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Click the **New Project** button in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill project details">
    Enter a project name and optional description, then click **Create**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin organizing docs">
    Use the sidebar to add pages, write documentation, or invite your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
