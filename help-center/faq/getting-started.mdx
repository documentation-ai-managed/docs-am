## Overview

To get started, sign in and create your first documentation project.

## Steps

1. Log in to your am dashboard.
2. Click **New Project** on the home screen.
3. Enter a name and optional description.
4. Click **Create**.

You can now add docs, organize pages, and invite collaborators from your project dashboard.
